Jewelry, Bridal and Accessories
Inspired by the Romance and the Spirit of the West The natural beauty of Montana offers a stunning backdrop of inspiration for Montana Silversmiths Sterling Lane Collection of authentic western themed jewelry, bridal and accessories. Sterling Lane jewelry and bridal offers timeless designs in solid sterling silver accentuated by high quality stones and Swarovski® Zirconia. To expand the Sterling Lane Collection, we are introducing bridal rings for women and men. In addition, Sterling Lane will offer cake toppers, accessories and personalized wedding party gifts to enhance wedding themes centered around the romance of the West. The intricate beauty of each piece lies in the depth of design details and its versatility to be worn, not only as a bridal item, but also as an accent piece of beauty and design for everyday elegance. Sterling Lane embodies authentic, genuine designs in jewelry, bridal, and accessories, for life changing events and moments to cherish.
